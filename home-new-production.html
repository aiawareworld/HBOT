Everything You Need to Know About Working With Us

What smart home devices do you offer?
We offer a comprehensive range including smart lighting, security cameras, door locks, thermostats, motion sensors, and complete automation systems tailored to your needs.
How long does installation take?
Installation typically takes 4-8 hours depending on the complexity of your system. Our expert technicians ensure everything is set up perfectly and provide complete training.
Do you provide warranty and support?
Yes, we provide comprehensive warranty coverage and 24/7 technical support. Our team is always ready to help with any questions or issues you might have.
Can I control everything from my phone?
Absolutely! Our H-Bot app gives you complete control over all your smart home devices from anywhere in the world. You can monitor, control, and automate everything remotely.
Is the system secure from hackers?
Security is our top priority. We use advanced encryption, secure protocols, and regular security updates to protect your smart home from any cyber threats.
